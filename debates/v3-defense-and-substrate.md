# v3 Defense and Physical Substrate Panel

This simulated panel asks what a software-only map of the agentic web misses.

## Archetype 1 — Defense technology operator

Defense is not just another customer vertical. It stress-tests autonomy, auditability, reliability, latency, permissions, and human control. Any serious agentic-web map should include national-security AI.

## Archetype 2 — Datacenter and energy strategist

The map overweights software and underweights physical constraint. Compute demand lands in real places: grids, substations, cooling systems, water basins, transmission queues, and datacenter campuses.

## Archetype 3 — Geopolitical supply-chain analyst

The substrate is strategic: semiconductors, lithography, minerals, refining, export controls, and sovereign cloud capacity. The map should show that the agentic web is conditioned by industrial policy and geopolitical chokepoints.

## Synthesis

v3 adds defense and physical substrate layers. These are not customer claims. They are structural preconditions.
