# Finding: the physical substrate is not optional

The agentic web is usually described as software: models, agents, browsers, tools, memory, evals, orchestration, and payments.

That is incomplete. The binding constraints are increasingly physical.

## Substrate dependencies

- chips and advanced packaging;
- GPUs and accelerators;
- datacenter siting and construction;
- power generation and grid interconnection;
- cooling and water;
- fiber, interconnects, and subsea cables;
- critical minerals and refining;
- export controls and industrial policy.

## Why this matters

A map that stops at the application layer treats the agentic web as if it were unconstrained by energy, supply chains, and geopolitics. The substrate is where much of the strategic competition actually happens.

## Atlas treatment

v3 adds a physical substrate layer. This layer does not imply customer relationships. It marks preconditions.
