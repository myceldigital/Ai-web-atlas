# Finding: defense and national-security AI are structurally underweighted

Any serious map of the agentic web should include defense and national-security AI.

Agentic systems are relevant to:

- intelligence workflows;
- command and control;
- logistics;
- cyber and electronic warfare;
- autonomy;
- battlefield decision support;
- simulation and planning.

## Why this matters

Defense is not merely another vertical. It is a forcing function for autonomy, reliability, auditability, latency, security, and human authorization.

## Atlas treatment

v3 adds a defense and national-security layer. It includes representative companies and systems but does not imply that every listed company is a customer of every other company.
