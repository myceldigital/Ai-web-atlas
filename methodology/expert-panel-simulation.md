# Expert Panel Simulation Methodology

AI Web Atlas uses simulated expert archetypes as a stress-testing method.

The panels are not real quotations from named public figures. They are structured analytical roles designed to disagree productively.

## Purpose

The method helps surface:

- missing layers;
- category errors;
- weak evidence;
- hidden incentives;
- commercial overclaiming;
- geopolitical or infrastructure blind spots.

## Archetypes

Useful archetypes include:

- open-source maintainer;
- AI infrastructure founder;
- frontier-lab product lead;
- venture market-map skeptic;
- defense technology operator;
- security researcher;
- datacenter and energy strategist;
- geopolitical supply-chain analyst;
- web standards architect.

## Process

1. Define the disputed artifact or map.
2. Assign archetypes with genuinely different incentives.
3. Ask each archetype what the map hides, overstates, or misclassifies.
4. Preserve disagreement rather than flattening it.
5. Convert conclusions into map changes only where the evidence supports them.
6. Record the debate and the resulting version.

## Limitations

This method is useful for critique, not for proprietary knowledge or factual authority. Claims still need evidence.
