# Evidence Standard

The atlas distinguishes between ecosystem presence and proven commercial relationship. A company appearing in a map is not evidence that it is a customer of any other company.

## Preferred evidence

1. Public customer case studies or product documentation
2. Company announcements and partner pages
3. Regulatory filings, procurement notices, or contract awards
4. Earnings-call transcripts or investor materials
5. Credible reporting with named sources

## Weak evidence

- Logo presence without explanatory text
- Unsourced blog posts
- Social-media claims
- Anonymous comments
- Visual proximity on a market map

## Confidence levels

- `high`: direct public evidence supports the classification.
- `medium`: multiple public signals support the classification but no direct proof exists.
- `low`: plausible but uncertain; should be treated as a hypothesis.
